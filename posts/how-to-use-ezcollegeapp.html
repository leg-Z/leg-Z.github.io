How the math works

Where the extra applications come from

01

Your existing work becomes the reference

The applications your counselor shaped — or the ones you finished yourself — are read in as the reference: the strategy, the voice, the reviewed profile. Everything we expand starts from that, not from a blank page.

02

Repeated work stops being repeated

Address, school codes, activity lists, family details — the fields that appear on every application get reused with review, not re-entered by hand each time.

03

Strong writing travels

A solid personal statement or Why-Major draft becomes a starting point you adapt per prompt, so the marginal cost of one more well-fit school drops sharply.

04

Review stays in the loop

Nothing submits itself. You accept, edit, or reject every suggestion — which is exactly why the extra volume stays at a quality your counselor would sign off on.
